# United States Information & National FOundation for Transparency (USINFO)

## Overview

The **U**nited **S**tates **I**nformation & **N**ational **FO**undation for Transparency (**USINFO**) is a proposed organization dedicated to enhancing transparency in the legislative process and amplifying civic participation nationwide. The goal is to create an open framework where citizens can easily access and understand government activities, policies, and decision-making processes.

## Concept

USINFO seeks to build a unified information system that cities can use under possible custom city features and serves as a transparent, accessible, and reliable resource for legislative processes. By offering citizens clear insights into legislation, government actions, and policy developments, we strive to encourage informed participation in democratic processes. Our mission is to amplify the voices of everyday people, ensuring that their concerns are heard and addressed.
